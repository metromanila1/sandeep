What Our Customers Say

Laxmi Narayana

"Metro Manila is a gem! The dishes are packed with authentic Filipino flavors, and every bite brings a taste of home. I tried their crispy pata, and it was the perfect balance of crunchy and tender. The staff is friendly and the ambiance truly makes you feel like you’re in a cozy nook in Manila. Can’t wait to bring friends here to experience it!"

Harsha

"If you’re looking for the best Filipino food, Metro Manila does not disappoint! The kare-kare was rich and creamy, and the bagoong served alongside was just the right amount of salty. We also ordered the sinigang, which had the perfect level of sourness to make it comforting and flavorful. The service was fantastic, and I love the modern Filipino décor — it’s a must-try spot in town!".

Amrith Sai

"Metro Manila is now my go-to for Filipino comfort food. Their lechon kawali is to die for, with crispy skin and juicy meat. The servings are generous, the price is reasonable, and the staff makes you feel welcome. Don’t skip the halo-halo for dessert! It’s a delightful finish to a perfect meal. Highly recommended for anyone craving Filipino cuisine!"
